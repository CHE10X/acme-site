# Lazarus — Recovery Readiness Overview

Lazarus helps you prove your backups **actually restore**. It evaluates whether your agent environment can be successfully rehydrated after failure, corruption, or upgrade mistakes. Lazarus is designed to surface gaps early—before an incident forces the test.

## What Lazarus Does

- Maps backup coverage for critical runtime surfaces
- Validates restore assumptions and readiness
- Highlights gaps that could block recovery
- Produces operator-ready readiness signals and guidance

## What Lazarus Does NOT Do

- No autonomous restoration
- No live-system mutation by default
- No destructive operations (no delete-by-default backups)

## Why Lazarus is Free

Recovery anxiety is universal and easy to verify. Lazarus is a trust wedge: it builds confidence and makes the rest of the reliability stack easier to adopt.
